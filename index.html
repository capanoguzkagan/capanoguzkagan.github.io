About

I am a Unity game developer based in Istanbul. I usually work across the full path from the first game idea to a release-ready build: core loop, input, UI flow, SDK integrations, optimization and build pipelines.

For me, a good game is not just one that works; it should also be easy to iterate on and flexible enough to keep improving. In idle and hybrid-casual projects, I have also worked actively on economy, progression, ad placement and remote config.

On the personal side, I am also working on Project NidoQ, a roguelike deckbuilder I am building in Unity.
